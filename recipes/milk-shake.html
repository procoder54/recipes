Milk Shake

Description

Milkshake is a cold, sweet and refreshing drink made with milk and some flavoring. The flavorings could be chocolate or fruit or combination of fruits.

Ingredients

  • Fruit Pieces (of choice) or chocolate
  • Cold milk - 1 cup
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Ice creame (if want)
  • Dry fruits

Steps

  1. Clean and peel the fruit that you use and cut into small chunks. Remove the seeds if present.
  2. Blend the fruit with all the other ingredients untill smooth.
  3. Pour in a glass, top with some dry fruits and it is ready to serve.
